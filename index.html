ABOUT

Parokya ni Edgar is a band from the Philippines formed in 1993 by a group of students from the Ateneo de Manila High School.
Parokya ni Edgar is omposed of Chito Miranda (lead vocals), Gab Chee Kee (rhythm guitar, vocals), Buwi Meneses (bass guitar),
Darius Semaña (lead guitar), Dindin Moreno (drums, percussion), and Vinci Montaner (backing vocals).
The band is known for their original rock novelty music and often adaptations of songs taken from popular songs both foreign and local.
The band is known and remembered for their original rock novelty songs and often humorous album covers.
The band uses a variety of styles from one song to another - alternative rock to pop rock, melancholy to rapcore, and so on -
while providing a humorous lightheartedness to their listeners.
Despite having "Edgar" in the band's name, none of the members go by this.

MEMBERS

Parokya ni Edgar Members

Chito Miranda

Chito Miranda, whose full name is Alfonso "Chito" Yanga Miranda Jr., is a Filipino singer, songwriter, and occasional actor born on February 7, 1976, in Manila, Philippines. He is best known as one of the founding members and the lead vocalist of the popular Filipino rock band Parokya ni Edgar.

For more information, visit Wikipedia.

Gab Chee Kee

Gab Chee Kee, full name Gabriel Ignatius Juan Chee Kee, is a Filipino guitarist, singer, and songwriter. He is known for composing and singing lead vocals on Parokya ni Edgar's sleeper hit "Your Song (My One and Only You)."

For more information, visit Wikipedia.

Buwi Meneses

Buwi Meneses, full name Buhawi Francia Meneses, is a Filipino bassist best known as a member of the band Parokya ni Edgar. He joined the band with childhood friends, including Chito Miranda.

For more information, visit Wikipedia.

Darius Semaña

Darius Gerard Laluna Semaña is the lead guitarist of the band Parokya ni Edgar. He is known for playing a Fender Stratocaster guitar and has been active since 1993.

For more information, visit Wikipedia.

Dindin Moreno

Ferdinand "Dindin" Alvarez Moreno is the drummer for Parokya ni Edgar. He has contributed to many of the band's albums since 1993.

For more information, visit Wikipedia.

Vinci Montaner

Vinci Montaner is a Filipino singer best known as a backup vocalist and founding member of the band Parokya ni Edgar. He is known for his humorous contributions to the band.

For more information, visit Wikipedia.
